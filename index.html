Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

Roza hypothesizes that the ideal bacterial species to make synthetic beef may be found in the belly button. To test her hypotheses Roza has sampled the navels of people across the country to identify bacterial species that colonize their belly buttons. Participants have been anonymized and assigned an ID number. This dashboard allows her research participants and fellow researchers to select an ID number and see what was found in their belly buttons.
